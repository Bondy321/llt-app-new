Loch Lomond Travel App Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 June 2026

This privacy policy explains how Loch Lomond Travel handles personal data in the LLT mobile app for passengers and drivers. The app is a closed-access travel operations app. Passengers sign in with a booking reference and booking email, and drivers sign in with a Loch Lomond Travel driver code. This policy covers the mobile app and app-related services, including Firebase, Expo push notifications, maps, safety support, group chat, and tour photo features.

How We Use Data

  • To verify passenger and driver access to the correct closed tour.
  • To provide itinerary, pickup, driver, manifest, map, chat, photo, notification, and safety features.
  • To send operational notifications and optional marketing-interest notifications that you enable.
  • To moderate user-generated chat and photo content, respond to reports, and protect passengers, drivers, and staff.
  • To diagnose app errors, crashes, login issues, sync issues, and backend problems.
  • To meet travel operations, safety, legal, accounting, insurance, and dispute-resolution requirements.

Third Parties And Processors

The app uses service providers to operate securely and reliably. These providers process app data only for the services they provide to Loch Lomond Travel.

  • Firebase and Google Cloud: authentication, Realtime Database, Cloud Storage, Cloud Functions, app backend operations, logs, diagnostics, and notification routing.
  • Expo services: Expo push notification delivery, EAS build/update services, and Expo Updates where used by the app release channel.
  • Apple services on iOS: App Store distribution, iOS permissions, camera/photo/location system features, Apple Maps/MapKit, phone, SMS, and local device services.
  • Google Maps services on Android where maps are used.
  • Hosting, operational support, and administration tools used by Loch Lomond Travel to run and support the app.

The current app code does not use advertising identifiers, IDFA, ad SDKs, data brokers, or cross-app tracking. If this changes, this policy and the App Store privacy information must be updated before release.

Legal Basis

Where UK data protection law applies, Loch Lomond Travel processes app data because it is necessary to provide travel services and app functionality, because Loch Lomond Travel has legitimate interests in operating safe and reliable tours, because certain records may be required for legal/accounting obligations, and because you consent to optional permissions such as notifications, camera, photos, and location.

Retention

  • App account records, push tokens, notification preferences, and device metadata are kept while needed for app access, tour operations, support, and reliability.
  • Chat messages, reactions, photos, captions, reports, and safety events are kept while needed for the tour, moderation, support, safety, legal, insurance, or dispute-resolution purposes.
  • Diagnostics, logs, crash snapshots, and operational alerts are kept only as long as needed to investigate reliability, security, and support issues.
  • Local offline cache remains on your device until you log out, delete your app account, uninstall the app, or the app clears it as part of normal operation.
  • Travel booking, accounting, legal, safety, and operational records may be retained outside the app for longer where Loch Lomond Travel is required or has a legitimate reason to keep them.

Account Deletion

You can request in-app account deletion from the account menu under Account & privacy and then Delete account. Deletion removes the app account used on that device, local app data, notification preferences, push-token state, local logs, offline tour packs, and app-owned tour content where the app can remove it. Owned group/private photos and app chat content are removed or marked deleted where possible. Driver pickup location may also be cleared for driver sessions.

Deleting the app account does not automatically erase travel booking, legal, accounting, insurance, safety, or operational records that Loch Lomond Travel needs to retain. To ask about access, correction, retention, or deletion of those records, email support@lochlomondtravel.com.

Permission And Consent Controls

  • Notifications can be skipped, disabled in the app notification preferences, or disabled in iOS/Android settings.
  • Location permission can be denied or changed in device settings. Safety reports and live sharing are optional except where the selected feature needs location.
  • Camera and photo-library permissions can be denied or changed in device settings. Photo upload and save features may not work without the relevant permission.
  • Marketing-interest notifications can be changed in the app notification preferences.
  • Local muting/hiding of reported chat or group photo content affects the current device and does not prevent Loch Lomond Travel from reviewing reports.

Children And Minors

The app is not directed at children and is not submitted as a Kids Category app. Some travel bookings may include minors as passengers. Where app or booking data relates to a minor, Loch Lomond Travel uses it only for travel operations, safety, support, legal, and accounting purposes, and not for cross-app tracking or third-party advertising.

Security

The app uses authenticated access, Firebase security rules, HTTPS, platform permission controls, local secure storage where appropriate, bounded logs, and redaction of sensitive identifiers in diagnostics. No system can be guaranteed completely secure, but Loch Lomond Travel takes reasonable steps to protect app data and limit access to people and service providers who need it.

International Processing

The app's service providers, including Google/Firebase and Expo, may process data in locations outside the United Kingdom or European Economic Area. Where this happens, Loch Lomond Travel relies on appropriate contractual, technical, and organizational safeguards provided by those processors.

Your Rights

You may ask to access, correct, delete, restrict, or receive a copy of personal data associated with the app, subject to legal and operational limits. You may also object to certain processing or withdraw consent for optional features by changing app or device settings. Contact support@lochlomondtravel.com.

Changes To This Policy

Loch Lomond Travel may update this policy when app features, service providers, legal requirements, or operational practices change. The latest version should be available from the app's Account & privacy screen and from the App Store privacy policy URL.
